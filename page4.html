John Arcilla


Romeo Gonzales Arcilla, known professionally as John Arcilla, was born on June 24, 1966 in Quezon City, Philippines. John Arcilla is a renowned Filipino actor known for his captivating performances on both stage and screen.


With a career spanning decades, he has become a pillar of Philippine cinema, earning accolades for his versatility and depth. Arcilla is best known for his portrayal of General Antonio Luna in the acclaimed historical drama "Heneral Luna," a role that garnered him widespread acclaim and international recognition.


His commanding presence and nuanced portrayal of complex characters have solidified his status as one of the most respected actors in the Philippines. Whether commanding the screen with his intense gaze or captivating audiences with his theatrical prowess, John Arcilla continues to leave an indelible mark on Philippine entertainment.


John Arcilla has received numerous awards throughout his illustrious career, celebrating his exceptional talent and contributions to Philippine cinema. He won Best Actor awards at the 78th Venice International Film Festival and the Gawad Urian Awards for his performance in On the Job: The Missing 8. He is the first Filipino and Southeast Asian to win the Volpi Cup for Best Actor.

Heneral Luna

"Heneral Luna" is a gripping historical drama set in the early 20th century Philippines, depicting the tumultuous period of the Philippine-American War. The film follows the fiery and uncompromising General Antonio Luna as he battles not only foreign invaders but also internal strife and political betrayal within his own ranks. "Heneral Luna" explores themes of patriotism, leadership, and the complexities of nation-building in the face of adversity.

Birdshot

"Birdshot" is a riveting Filipino thriller that weaves together the lives of a young farm girl and a rookie policeman in a remote rural area. When the girl unknowingly commits a crime by shooting a rare Philippine eagle, it sets off a chain of events that expose the dark underbelly of corruption and injustice in their community. As the policeman investigates, he uncovers layers of deception and violence, leading to a gripping climax that challenges notions of morality and accountability.

On The Job

"On the Job" is a gritty Filipino crime thriller that delves into the underworld of contract killings and political corruption. Set in the bustling streets of Manila, the film follows two parallel storylines: one centered on an ambitious young hitman and his seasoned mentor, and the other on a dedicated law enforcement officer determined to bring them to justice.
